Our Story

Refactory is a Company Limited by Guarantee that was formed by four partners; Laboremus Uganda Ltd, Fontes Foundation, Youth With A Mission UK, and Clarke International University. It has been supported by funding from the Norwegian Agency for International Development (NORAD) and other donors. It is focused on equipping learners with skills to enable them get into gainful employemnt.

Our Vision

A vibrant and growing African tech sector made up of individuals empowered with the right tech skills.

Our Mission

To be the industry’s preferred skilling partner for tech talent.

Refactory is a Tech skilling Academy. We are the industry’s blueprint for hiring software talent.
